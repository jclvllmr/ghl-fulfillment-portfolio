# ComfortFlow HVAC Services – Project Brief

## Project Overview

ComfortFlow HVAC Services is a fictional residential heating and cooling company based in Austin, Texas.

This portfolio project simulates a complete GoHighLevel client fulfillment engagement, from initial planning through website development, CRM setup, automation, quality assurance, and launch preparation.

## Business Problem

The business currently has no centralized system for:

- Capturing website leads
- Following up with new inquiries
- Scheduling service appointments
- Managing sales opportunities
- Recovering missed calls
- Sending appointment reminders
- Tracking leads through the sales process

## Project Objective

Build a complete GoHighLevel system that helps the business:

- Generate and capture leads
- Respond to inquiries quickly
- Book more appointments
- Track leads through a CRM pipeline
- Automate SMS and email follow-ups
- Reduce missed opportunities
- Provide a professional customer experience

## Planned Deliverables

- Responsive GoHighLevel website
- Contact and estimate request forms
- Appointment booking calendar
- CRM pipeline
- Custom fields and tags
- New lead follow-up workflow
- Missed call text-back workflow
- Appointment confirmation and reminders
- Estimate follow-up automation
- QA checklist
- Launch checklist
- SOP documentation
- Loom project walkthrough
